Brief Introduction to the School

Over 50+ years of unwavering trust in the field of education! Established in 1967, Shri Asaldev Inter College has been providing high-quality education to children in rural and semi-urban areas of the Bhadar block (Peeparpur) in the Amethi district. Our school is a co-educational institution recognized by the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (UP Board), offering excellent education from classes 6th to 12th. We emphasize not only bookish knowledge but also character building and the holistic development of our students.
