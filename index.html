Brief Introduction to the School

Over 59+ years of unwavering trust in the field of education! Established in 1967, Shri Asaldev Inter College has been providing high-quality education to children in rural and semi-urban areas of Piparpur, Amethi. Our school is a co-educational institution recognized by the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (UP Board), offering excellent education from classes 6th to 12th.
