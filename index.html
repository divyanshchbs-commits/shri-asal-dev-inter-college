Brief Introduction to the School

Over 59+ years of unwavering trust in the field of education! Established in 1967, Shri Asaldev Inter College has been providing high-quality education to children in rural and semi-urban areas of Piparpur, Amethi. Our school is a co-educational institution recognized by the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (UP Board), offering excellent education from classes 6th to 12th. We emphasize not only bookish knowledge but also character building and the holistic development of our students.

Principal's Message

From the Principal's Desk

"It is my proud privilege and a matter of immense pride to welcome you to Shri Asal Dev Inter College, Piparpur, Amethi. For more than five decades, since our establishment in 1967, this institution has been selflessly igniting the flame of education in our rural area, ensuring that quality knowledge and strong values reach every student.

“शीलं परं भूषणम्” (Character is the highest virtue) is our guiding principle. Our primary objective for students from classes 6 to 12 (Intermediate) is not just to impart textbook knowledge, but to mold them into disciplined, cultured, and responsible citizens. As a co-educational institution recognized by the UP Board, we provide equal opportunities for both boys and girls to progress, showcase their talent, and grow under the guidance of our experienced teachers.

We are strongly committed to working collaboratively with parents to nurture individuals who possess deep patriotism, moral discipline, and respect for family values. We encourage our students to persevere in the face of challenges and always adopt a ‘never give up’ spirit.

This golden journey of over 59 years has been made possible by the unwavering trust and strong partnership among the school management, staff, and parents. We welcome your ideas and continuous support in making this generation ready for a better world.

I pray for the bright and successful future of all my students."

Dr. Lal Singh

Principal
Shri Asal Dev Inter College, Piparpur, Amethi (U.P.)
