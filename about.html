About Our Institution

Nurturing Excellence, Character, and Wisdom Since Inception

Our Legacy & History

Shri Asal Dev Inter College, located in the peaceful and culturally rich region of Piparpur, Amethi, stands as a beacon of academic brilliance and comprehensive personal development. Founded with a visionary outlook to provide high-quality, modern education grounded in traditional ethical values, our institution has consistently empowered generations of students to excel in various spheres of life.

We believe in providing an inclusive environment where intellectual curiosity is sparked, and students are given ample opportunities to discover their hidden potentials. Guided by our sacred motto "शीलं परं भूषणम्" (Character is the highest virtue), our dedicated faculty works relentlessly to cultivate discipline, critical thinking, and leadership qualities in every single child.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To transform into a world-class premier learning community that fosters outstanding academic innovation, strong moral uprightness, and an unyielding sense of social responsibility among rural and urban students alike.

Our Mission: To offer accessible, cutting-edge infrastructural resources, holistic educational programs, and a safe, nurturing environment that prepares future citizens to tackle dynamic global challenges with extreme confidence and empathy.
