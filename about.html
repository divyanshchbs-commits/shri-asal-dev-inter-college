About Our Institution

Shaping young minds and fostering academic excellence since 1967 in rural and semi-urban landscapes.

Our Rich History & Heritage

Shri Asal Dev Inter College, situated in Piparpur, Amethi, was established in 1967 with a vision to provide high-quality education to students in rural and semi-urban areas. Named after the revered Shri Asal Dev Ji, the institution has stood as a beacon of knowledge and moral character for generations.

From its humble beginnings with just a few classrooms, the college has transformed into a premier inter college, offering comprehensive educational programs in Science, Arts, and Commerce streams under the curriculum guidelines of the state board.

Over the past five decades, our alumni have gone on to excel in various professional domains including civil services, medicine, engineering, and defense, keeping the school motto "Sheelam Param Bhushanam" close to their hearts.

Our Vision

To be an educational institution of excellence that nurtures young individuals into intellectually competent, morally upright, and socially responsible citizens who can positively contribute to the growth of our nation.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality modern education embedded with rich traditional values. We aim to create an inclusive learning ecosystem that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and robust physical development.

Our Core Values

Character (Sheelam)

We place highest emphasis on integrity, modesty, and good behavior as true ornaments of education.

Excellence

Striving continuously to raise the bar in academics, sports, and co-curricular domains.

Inclusivity

Ensuring that every child, regardless of socio-economic background, receives equal opportunities.

Discipline

Fostering self-regulation and dedication that helps students navigate challenges in adult life.
