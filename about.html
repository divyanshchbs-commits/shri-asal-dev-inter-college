About Our Institution

Nurturing minds, building character, and achieving excellence since 1967.

Our Rich History

Shri Asal Dev Inter College was founded in the year 1967 with a vision to provide high-quality education to the rural youths of Piparpur, Amethi, and surrounding areas. Named after the revered local inspiration, the college has grown from a humble setup into a premier educational center of the district.

Over the past 59+ years, the institution has consistently maintained high academic standards, helping thousands of students excel in board examinations, higher studies, and various professional careers across India.

Our Vision

To emerge as a benchmark institution that blends traditional values with modern scientific education, enabling every child to discover and fulfill their true potential.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and holistic education. We focus on intellectual discipline, spiritual development, and physical fitness to prepare responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Our Core Values

We believe that education is incomplete without character building. Our student core philosophies revolve around four primary pillars:

Excellence

Striving for the best in academics and co-curricular fields.

Character (Sheelam)

Upholding high moral values and deep respect for elders.

Inclusivity

Equal growth opportunities for every child without any bias.

Discipline

Punctuality, consistency, and orderly behavior in school life.

Campus & Infrastructure

Our college boasts a large green campus designed to provide an optimal learning environment away from town distractions.

Smart Classrooms

Equipped with audio-visual learning aids for tough concepts.

Science Laboratories

Fully functional practical labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

Rich Library

Houses thousands of academic books, journals, and news references.
