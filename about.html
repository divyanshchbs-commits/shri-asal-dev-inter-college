About Our Institution

Nurturing minds, building character, and achieving excellence since 1967.

Our Rich History & Heritage

Shri Asal Dev Inter College was founded in the year 1967 with a distinct vision to provide high-quality education to the rural youths of Piparpur, Amethi, and its neighboring regions. Named after the revered local inspiration, the college has steadily grown from a humble academic setup into a premier educational center of the district.

Over the past 59+ years, the institution has consistently maintained exceptional standards, helping thousands of students excel in board examinations, higher university studies, and various distinguished professional careers across India.

Our Vision

To emerge as a benchmark institution that blends traditional moral values with modern scientific education, enabling every child to discover and fulfill their true potential.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and holistic education. We focus on intellectual discipline, spiritual development, and physical fitness to prepare responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest quality in academics, co-curricular skills, and institutional growth benchmarks.

Character (Sheelam)

Upholding deep moral ethics, honesty, personal integrity, and profound respect for societal elders.

Inclusivity

Providing equal, fair development opportunities to every single student without any social or economic bias.

Discipline

Inculcating perfect punctuality, mutual respect, self-control, and an orderly behavior pattern in school life.

Our Campus Infrastructure

Equipped with modern visual projectors and audio aids to make regular abstract concepts highly engaging and easy to absorb.

Science Laboratories

Fully functional, well-stocked physics, chemistry, and biology labs designed to give practical hands-on experience safely.

Rich Library
